Additional Actions to Promote Lanwell

On top of search engine optimization (SEO), Lanwell Publishing could take advantage of several other methods to improve reach and ranking.

Reciprocal Link Agreements

By partnering with other websites, being featured in the news, or by blogs that leave reviews linking to our site, search engines rank this website higher based on the number of sites and quality of those that link to us.

Newsletters

Sending out updates about new arrivals, best practices for teaching ESL, personal recommendations based on level, etc. would make great content for a newsletter that users such as schools, professors, and students can opt-in to keep engagement high.

Affiliate Programs

Other websites or content creators can link to our website, promoting the textbooks and manuals while getting a small comission; or, our website can promote e.g.: Amazon products related to our niche and we get a comission after a purchase as well as the prestige from associating with such a well-established company.
